Macro markers

Macro Markers provides a UX framework for displaying multiple sources of timed metadata to the viewer, allowing the user to navigate to a specific marked moment in the video. Video chapters is the first phase of Macro markers.

Watch feed

Watch Feed is a reorganization of the space below the player on the YouTube mobile app. This project aims to make it easier to add new, appealing content to the watch page (Ads, Posts, Shorts, Surveys, promotions etc.) while improving user comprehension with a simpler information architecture.

Double tap to seek

Double tap to seek enables users to double tap the left and right side of the screen to skip 10 seconds. It solves the usability issue that is innate of video - fine seeking long form content.

Autoplay

After video ends, the next video automatically plays after a countdown. The goal is to remove the need for users to make a decision about what to watch next, while giving them control. On fullscreen, related videos reveal if a user cancels/disables autoplay.

Facebook for business

Facebook provides a series of products to help business owners to grow their audience - Promoted Posts is on-timeline ad creation, originally aimed at small business that don't have the time to use the full-featured ads interfaces. It has very simple set of options, which makes it easy for Page Admins to create ads and gain quick insights.
